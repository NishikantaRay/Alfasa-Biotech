Welcome to Alfasa Biotech

At Alfasa Biotech, we constantly work towards ensuring access to high quality and affordable medicines to support patients in need. Which is why, we have been trusted by health care professionals and patients. Caring For Life has always been and continues to remain, our guiding purpose. We develop innovative medicines that greatly improve health outcomes for patients with an unremitting focus on quality and affordability.


We care deeply for patients and customers, their families, our employees, and our communities. We strive to always do the right thing, pursuing the highest standards in quality, compliance, safety, and performance. We embrace diverse backgrounds and perspectives and treat everyone equally, with dignity and respect, allowing us all to achieve our best.

Why Choose US

  • Our purpose

    • Our purpose is profound, and our path is clear. We embrace the responsibility of making a remarkable impact on people's lives through the innovative medicines and solutions we create together. This is driven by our compassion for people, commitment to innovation and inclusion, service to the community, and uncompromising integrity at the heart of everything we do.

  • Our commitment

    • Our commitment to health doesn’t stop with our medicines. Each day, we work to deliver sustainable solutions that improve the health of our business and the health of humankind. Our core strength lies in identifying, researching, and creating a unique lifestyle modifying nutraceutical and therapeutic range of products. Alfasa Biotech is a name to reckon with in the therapy areas of women's health, reproductive health, joint, bone and tendon health, pain management, cardiodiabetic health, immune health and general health.
